## KEY FINDINGS

1. **All 20 operators have Baxtel pages.** Two required non-obvious slugs:
   - Digital Realty: `digital-realty-trust` (not `digital-realty`)
   - Aligned: `aligned-energy` (not `aligned-data-centers`)

2. **Total facilities across all 20 operators on Baxtel: ~1,588**

3. **Largest Baxtel portfolios by facility count:**
   - Digital Realty Trust: 278
   - Equinix: 230+ (claims 313)
   - Vantage: 140
   - QTS: 118
   - CyrusOne: 118
   - STACK Infrastructure: 117

4. **Northern Virginia dominates** with 591 total facilities from 77 providers, far exceeding Dallas (253) and Phoenix (180).

5. **Baxtel data quality notes:**
   - Includes facilities at all stages (operational, under construction, planned)
   - Some operators have subsidiary brands listed separately (e.g., Aligned's ODATA, Digital Realty's Ascenty/Teraco)
   - Facility counts may include individual buildings within a campus as separate entries
   - Hyperscaler-owned facilities (AWS, Azure, Google, Meta) appear on region pages but not as standalone operator pages in this audit
