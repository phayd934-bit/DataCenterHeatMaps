## Official Legal Databases
- **UK**: legislation.gov.uk (primary law), gov.uk (guidance/policy)
- **Norway**: lovdata.no (statutes + forskrifter), regjeringen.no (policy/strategy), skatteetaten.no (tax rates)
- **Finland**: finlex.fi (statutes), valtioneuvosto.fi (govt decisions), energiavirasto.fi (energy authority), avi.fi (regional permits)
- **Iceland**: government.is (legislation PDFs + topic pages), althingi.is (parliament), orkustofnun.is -> uos.is (new Energy Agency from Jan 2025)
- **Denmark**: retsinformation.dk (all law), mst.dk/eng.mst.dk (environment agency), ens.dk (energy agency), bygningsreglementet.dk (building code)

## Key Regulations Found (April 2026)
- **UK**: ESOS (SI 2014/1643 + SI 2023/1182), SECR (SI 2018/1155), Part L (Approved Doc L), EPR 2016 (SI 2016/1154), F-gas (SI 2023/1161), CCA data centre sector, CNI designation (Sep 2024), NSIP expansion (Dec 2024)
- **Norway**: Forskrift om datasenter FOR-2024-12-18-3313 (0.5MW registration), Data Centre Strategy June 2025 (2.5GWh audit + 2MW waste heat CBA), Forurensningsloven (1981), Vannressursloven (2000), TEK17, Energiloven (1990), electricity tax reduced rate removed 2023
- **Finland**: Excise Duty Act 1260/1996 (Cat II->I from Jul 2026), Energy Efficiency Act 1429/2014, Env Protection Act 527/2014, EED reporting via Energiavirasto, State aid + registration (Dec 2025 decision), Land Use & Building Act 132/1999
- **Iceland**: Electricity Act 65/2003, EIA Act 106/2000, Hygiene/Pollution Prevention Act 7/1998, Water Management Act, Building Reg 112/2012, Workplace Safety Act 46/1980, no DC-specific tax incentive (structural low price advantage)
- **Denmark**: Lov nr 1775/2025 (elafgift to 0.8 øre for 2026-27), Varmeforsyningsloven (surplus heat cap removed Jul 2025), Miljøbeskyttelsesloven Ch.5, Vandforsyningsloven (LBK 1149/2024), BR18, Klimaloven 2020, DEA Efficiency Roadmap

## Data Gaps
- Iceland has no DC-specific energy efficiency or PUE reporting mandate (not EU member, EED does not apply directly)
- Norway data centre strategy (June 2025) is policy, not yet codified as legislation — watch for forskrifter implementing waste heat CBA requirement
- Denmark surplus heat price cap removal (Jul 2025): specific legislative vehicle not yet on retsinformation.dk at time of research
- Finland state aid scheme details still being prepared (multi-ministry coordination, autumn 2026 target)
