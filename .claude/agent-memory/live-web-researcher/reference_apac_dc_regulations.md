## Japan
- **METI energy efficiency policy**: https://www.meti.go.jp/english/policy/energy_environment/energy_efficiency/index.html
- **Act on Rationalizing Energy Use** revised April 2023 — 1,500 kL threshold for designated business operators
- **Building Energy Efficiency Act** (MLIT) mandatory for all new buildings from April 2025
- **Environmental Impact Assessment Act**: https://www.env.go.jp/en/laws/policy/assess/index.html — DC not explicitly listed
- **Water Pollution Control Law**: 50 m3/day discharge threshold, 24 substances for human health
- **GX Promotion Act**: 20 trillion yen transition bonds; emissions trading from FY2026
- **Economic Security Promotion Act**: 72.5 billion yen subsidies for 5 cloud/AI projects (April 2024)

## South Korea
- **Energy Use Rationalization Act**: https://law.go.kr/LSW/lsInfoP.do?lsiSeq=98468 (English)
- **Framework Act on Carbon Neutrality**: https://law.go.kr/LSW//lsInfoP.do?lsiSeq=235581 — 40% GHG cut by 2030
- **Water Environment Conservation Act**: https://law.go.kr/LSW/lsInfoP.do?lsiSeq=218199 — 51 effluent parameters
- **GIR emissions reporting**: https://www.gir.go.kr/eng/ — K-ETS trading scheme
- **MSIT AI computing centers**: https://www.msit.go.kr/eng/ — KRW 18.6T budget 2024

## Singapore (highest complexity — 72)
- **GMDC:2024**: https://www.imda.gov.sg/how-we-can-help/bca-imda-green-mark-for-data-centres-scheme — PUE 1.25 at full load for DC-CFA2
- **SS 697:2023 Tropical DC Standard**: raise temps to 26+ deg C, 2-5% savings per deg C
- **SS 715:2025 IT Energy Efficiency**: 30% savings target, equipment rated for 35 deg C
- **Energy Conservation Act 2012**: 54 TJ threshold → mandatory energy manager, annual reports
- **Cooling tower regs**: NEA registration, Legionella control
- **PUB water efficiency**: 60,000 m3/year threshold → mandatory WEMP
- **DC-CFA2 (2025)**: 200+ MW, PUE 1.25, 50% green energy, Platinum Green Mark required

## Australia (lowest complexity — 52)
- **NABERS DC ratings**: https://www.nabers.gov.au/ratings/spaces-we-rate/data-centres — voluntary 1-6 stars
- **NCC 2022 Section J**: https://ncc.abcb.gov.au/ — energy efficiency for new commercial buildings
- **NGER/Safeguard Mechanism**: 100,000 tCO2e threshold, 4.9% annual baseline decline
- **CBD program expansion**: $20M investment, data centres likely included — will make NABERS mandatory
- **Water regulated at state level** — varies by jurisdiction
