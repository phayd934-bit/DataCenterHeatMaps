## Primary Sources for Sovereign AI / National Compute DCs

### Government Portals
- **Canada ISED**: https://ised-isde.canada.ca/site/ised/en/enabling-large-scale-sovereign-ai-data-centres
- **EuroHPC JU AI Factories**: https://www.eurohpc-ju.europa.eu/ai-factories_en (19 sites selected, 13 antennas)
- **UK AIRR**: UKRI opportunity pages for Isambard-AI and Dawn
- **Taiwan NSTC**: via NCHC/ASUS case studies
- **IndiaAI Mission**: https://indiaai.gov.in/hub/indiaai-compute-capacity
- **Singapore IMDA**: DC-CFA2 capacity call (200MW, PUE < 1.30)
- **Saudi SDAIA**: via Saudi Gazette, Arab News for Hexagon DC
- **South Korea MSIT**: Samsung SDS consortium via Korean news (SeDaiLy, AsiaE)

### Vendor/Partner Blogs (Sovereign AI Announcements)
- **NVIDIA Blog**: blogs.nvidia.com/blog/ -- sovereign AI keyword; covers ABCI, JUPITER, Taiwan, France, Indonesia, UAE
- **HPE Newsroom**: HammerHAI, Isambard-AI, ABCI 3.0 announcements
- **Eviden/Atos**: JUPITER, Leonardo, MareNostrum 5 BullSequana specs

### Industry Publications
- **DCD**: Best single source for MW, cooling, timeline facts across all regions
- **HPCwire**: Off-the-wire section captures EuroHPC, NVIDIA, HPE press releases same-day
- **Introl Blog**: Excellent country-level AI infra analysis (South Korea, India, Malaysia, Singapore, France)
- **Data Centre Magazine**: Good for Australia, UK, Saudi sovereign DC coverage
