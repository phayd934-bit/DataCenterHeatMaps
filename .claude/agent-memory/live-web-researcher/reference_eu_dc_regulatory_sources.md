## Official Government Legal Databases for DC Regulations

### Spain (ES)
- **BOE.es** (Boletin Oficial del Estado): https://www.boe.es/
- Key laws: RDL 1/2001 (Water), RDL 1/2016 (IPPC), RD 314/2006+732/2019 (CTE building code), RD 390/2021 (energy certs), Ley 50/1985 (regional incentives)
- EED 2023/1791 transposition: NOT complete as of Apr 2026. EU Reg 2024/1364 directly applicable.
- MITECO data center page: https://www.miteco.gob.es/en/energia/eficiencia/centros-de-datos/normativa.html

### Italy (IT)
- **Normattiva.it**: https://www.normattiva.it/ (consolidated texts)
- **Gazzetta Ufficiale**: https://www.gazzettaufficiale.it/ (original publications)
- Key laws: DLgs 102/2014 (energy efficiency), DLgs 152/2006 (environment), DPR 380/2001 (building), RD 1775/1933 (water), Art.38 DL 19/2024 (Transizione 5.0)
- EED transposition: missed Oct 2025 deadline, draft under consultation
- ENEA manages DC reporting portal

### Poland (PL)
- **ISAP**: https://isap.sejm.gov.pl/ (Internetowy System Aktow Prawnych)
- Key laws: Ustawa o efektywnosci energetycznej (Dz.U.2024.1047), Prawo budowlane (Dz.U.2024.725), Prawo wodne (Dz.U.2024.1087), Prawo ochrony srodowiska (Dz.U.2025.647), Ustawa o wspieraniu nowych inwestycji (Dz.U.2018.1162)
- EED transposition: NOT complete as of Apr 2026

### Portugal (PT)
- **DRE**: https://dre.pt/ (Diario da Republica Eletronico)
- Key laws: DL 118/2013 (SCE/RECS energy certs), Lei 58/2005 (Water Law), DL 226-A/2007 (water use), DL 75/2015 (LUA single env licensing), CFI/RFAI/SIFIDE (tax codes)
- EED transposition: pending
- LUA single-portal approach is notably streamlined

### Switzerland (CH)
- **Fedlex**: https://www.fedlex.admin.ch/ (requires JavaScript for content)
- Key laws: EnG SR 730.0 (Energy Act), EnV SR 730.01 (Energy Ordinance), CO2 Act (revised 2024), GSchG SR 814.20 (Waters Protection), USG SR 814.01 (Environment), RPG SR 700 (Spatial Planning)
- NOT in EU: no EED, no EU Reg 2024/1364. Grossverbraucher (>500 MWh) programme applies instead.
- Cantonal variation is significant — building permits, tax rates, and water permits are cantonal

### Cross-cutting
- EU Delegated Regulation 2024/1364 (DC reporting) is directly applicable in all EU member states without national transposition
- All four EU countries (ES/IT/PL/PT) missed or are late on full EED 2023/1791 transposition as of Apr 2026
