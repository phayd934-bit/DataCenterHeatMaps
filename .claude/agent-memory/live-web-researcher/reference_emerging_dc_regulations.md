## Saudi Arabia
- CST Data Center Services Regulations (effective 2024-01-01): cst.gov.sa — 4-tier registration, sustainability plans mandatory
- SBC 601 energy conservation code: seec.gov.sa — HVAC efficiency for commercial buildings
- SASO 2874: saso.gov.sa — large AC energy efficiency standards
- Environment Regulation (Royal Decree M/165, 2020): mewa.gov.sa — NCEC permits required for construction and operation
- Water Law (Royal Decree M/159, 2020): laws.boe.gov.sa — water extraction permits critical in water-scarce Saudi
- Cloud Computing SEZ: misa.gov.sa — Zakat exemption, customs suspension, effective Apr 2026

## UAE
- Federal Decree-Law No. 11/2024 (climate): uaelegislation.gov.ae/en/legislations/2558 — emissions reporting mandatory, 0.5M ton threshold, effective May 2025
- Sustainable Digital Transformation Framework: uaelegislation.gov.ae — gov procurement preference for green DCs
- TDRA National DC Strategy 2023: tdra.gov.ae — ISO 27001/50001 required
- Al Sa'fat (Dubai): dm.gov.ae — mandatory green building, minimum Silver rating
- Estidama Pearl (Abu Dhabi): jawdah.qcc.abudhabi.ae — mandatory 1 Pearl, 2 for gov
- Corporate Tax 9% but 0% in free zones: strong incentive for DC investment

## India
- Infrastructure status (Oct 2022): DEA notification, cheap long-term financing
- Energy Conservation Amendment Act 2022: powermin.gov.in — carbon credits, designated consumers, PAT scheme
- ECBC 2017: beeindia.gov.in — 100kW+ commercial buildings
- EIA Notification 2006: environmentclearance.nic.in — 20,000 m2 threshold triggers clearance, DCs not explicitly listed
- CGWA NOC: cgwa.mowr.gov.in — groundwater extraction permits
- Tax holiday to 2047 (Budget 2025-26): Entry 13C Schedule IV IT Act 2025
- DPDPA 2023: meity.gov.in — potential data localization driving domestic DC demand

## Brazil
- ANATEL CP 48/2025 + Res 780/2025: informacoes.anatel.gov.br — first DC-specific conformity assessment regime
- REDATA (MP 1318/2025): planalto.gov.br — zero PIS/COFINS/IPI on DC equipment, 100% renewable required
- CONAMA Res 237/1997: conama.mma.gov.br — environmental licensing (LP-LI-LO)
- Climate Policy (Lei 12187/2009): planalto.gov.br
- Distributed Generation (Lei 14300/2022): planalto.gov.br — on-site solar with net metering
- SUDENE/SUDAM: up to 75% IRPJ reduction, Manaus FTZ benefits to 2073

## Turkey
- BTK Authorization (Law 5809): btk.gov.tr — ISS/AIH authorization required, DC-specific law pending
- Energy Efficiency Law 5627: mevzuat.gov.tr — energy audits for large consumers
- BEP-TR (Building Energy Performance): mevzuat.gov.tr/13594 — NZEB for 2000 m2+, B-class EPC min
- Environmental Law 2872 + EIA Reg 2014: mevzuat.gov.tr — cevre izni required
- Presidential Decree 9903 (May 2025): new incentive system, 3MW+ = Priority Investment, PUE max 1.65
- TDZ Law 4691: CIT exemption on R&D profits within technology zones
- DSI Groundwater Law 167: water extraction permits
