## Verified Liquid Cooling Operators (US)

**Flexential** — Deployed liquid-cooled high-density cabinets (>80 kW/rack) for CoreWeave in Plano TX (13 MW, April 2025). Hillsboro 3 OR has built-in liquid cooling. Douglasville GA 1 and 2 both have liquid cooling. Strong AI/HPC deployment partner.

**Applied Digital** — Polaris Forge 1 in Ellendale ND uses closed-loop waterless direct-to-chip cooling. PUE 1.18, WUE near zero. Primary vendor: custom closed-loop system. CoreWeave tenant.

**Edged Energy** — Aurora IL (ORD01-1, 24 MW). ThermalWorks proprietary closed-loop waterless system. Liquid cooling up to 400+ kW/rack. Designed PUE 1.15, Chicago mPUE 1.086. Portfolio-wide zero water cooling.

**Equinix** — Announced Dec 2023 rollout of direct-to-chip liquid cooling to 100+ IBX data centers across 45+ metros including Washington D.C. and Silicon Valley. CDU pilots at Ashburn Co-Innovation Facility in 2024. Global PUE 1.39.

**Iron Mountain** — 11 liquid cool deployments in Q4 2025, 5 in NYC metro area (includes NJE-1 Edison NJ). PUE as low as 1.2 at NJE-1. 2024 Sustainability Performance Overview PDF available at s204.q4cdn.com.

**Aligned Data Centers** — Launched DeltaFlow liquid cooling (Jan 2024) supporting 300 kW/rack at PHX-01 Phoenix AZ. Arizona, Texas, Oregon, Illinois, Virginia, Utah locations.

**CoreWeave** — Not an operator but primary tenant driving liquid cooling deployments. All 2025+ facilities designed with liquid cooling, supporting 130 kW racks. GB200 NVL72 = 85% liquid/15% air. Active in: Ellendale ND (Applied Digital), Plano TX (Flexential), Hillsboro OR (Flexential), Douglasville GA (Flexential).

**Vantage Data Centers** — Supports up to 50+ kW/rack with liquid-to-rack cooling. Adaptive cooling architecture. Portfolio PUE under 1.3, targeting low 1.2.

**Why:** These are the highest-priority targets for a cooling technology sales motion. Flexential, Applied Digital, and Edged are the most operationally committed. Equinix has 100+ sites in rollout.
